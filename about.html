ABOUT ME

I was born and raised in Nova Scotia, and always had a passion for video games and technology. Pursuing an education through NSCC Institute of Technology was an easy choice. During my years of study, I had the opportunity to learn a wide range of programming languages such as PHP and Python as well as Web Design.

Frameworks like Laravel are a joy to work with. I appreciate how much work went into them to make development so much easier for programmers like me. I’ve always felt like programming is a bit like a puzzle that is asking to be solved, you just have to know how the pieces fit together.

My nights can often be filled with family and friends enjoying a lively game of Dungeons and Dragons or Magic the Gathering. When I’m not gaming, I love to go camping in some of Nova Scotia's beautiful provincial parks or kayaking down a peaceful river.
